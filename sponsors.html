Why sponsor the Optical Biology Conference?

The Optical Biology Conference is an annual, student-led retreat that brings together early-career researchers and group leaders working at the interface of optical imaging and biology. The event is intentionally small and interactive (≈60–100 attendees), making it an excellent opportunity for sponsors to meet engaged students, postdocs and academic staff in an informal setting.

  • Targeted exposure: direct engagement with next-generation imaging scientists — high-quality conversations at posters, coffee breaks and demos.
  • Flexible options: financial support, in-kind contributions (equipment demos, loans), travel bursaries, or event-specific sponsorship (dinner, poster prizes).
  • Transparency: we publish a short post-event report for sponsors summarising attendance and impact.

Sponsorship packages

All packages include: logo on the sponsors slide shown between sessions, acknowledgement during opening & closing remarks, and a short post-event report. Benefits are scalable and can be combined (e.g., Gold + Poster Prize).

LevelSuggested contributionCore benefits
Gold €3,000 Prominent logo on website & programme; logo on event banner; exhibition table in poster area; 10–15 min speaking slot or equipment demo during Industry Day; inclusion in welcome pack; 2 complimentary event passes.
Silver €1,500 Logo on website & printed programme; acknowledgement during event; inclusion of literature in welcome pack; 1 complimentary representative.
Bronze €750 Logo in programme and on sponsor slide; acknowledgement in the programme booklet.
Supporter €300 Name listed on the website and programme footer; acknowledgement on sponsor slide.

À la carte & in-kind options

  • Welcome Dinner Sponsor — €1,200 (logo in dinner materials, brief welcome acknowledgement)
  • Conference Dinner Sponsor — €1,500 (logo at dinner, event acknowledgement)
  • Industry Day Session — €1,000 (slot for up to four short industry talks or demos)
  • Poster Prize — €300–€500 (branded certificate, announcement at closing)
  • Travel bursaries — €400–€600 per student (named bursary on website/programme)
  • In-kind — equipment loans / demo kits / branded materials / catering support (benefits negotiable)

We love bespoke packages — tell us the visibility or engagement you want and we will design a custom package.

Visibility & engagement

Sample sponsor visibility depending on level:

  • On-site: exhibition table space in poster hall (Gold / Silver), branded slide between sessions, poster prizes, demo room access for hands-on equipment.
  • Online & print: logo on the conference one-pager, PDF programme, and post-event report.
  • People: short speaking/demo slot (Gold/Industry Day), direct conversations with students and PIs.

Process, deadlines & invoicing

Deadline for confirmed sponsors: ideally three months before the event to ensure logo placement and programme printing. Exact deadlines will be confirmed once dates are finalised.

Invoice & payment: we can issue invoices via the host institute or an organising account. When you confirm a package please indicate billing details and any purchase-order requirements and we will send a formal sponsorship agreement and invoice.

Post-event report: within 4–6 weeks after the conference we will supply a short report summarising attendance and sponsor visibility (logo use, talk/demo attendance, and poster/prize winners where applicable).
