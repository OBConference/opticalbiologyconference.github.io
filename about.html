The Optical Biology PhD Programme

The Optical Biology PhD programme (Wellcome-funded) trains researchers at the intersection of optics, microscopy, computation and biology. Students receive interdisciplinary training and access to state-of-the-art imaging facilities. The conference is run each year by cohorts of these students together with a local host institute.

Find programme details at opticalbiology.org.
