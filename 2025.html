Overview

The Optical Biology Conference 2025 was organised jointly by UCL LMCB and VIB–KU Leuven. The meeting brought together PhD students, postdocs, PIs and industry partners for a programme of keynote talks, student presentations, poster sessions and an industry day.

Key figures

  • Dates: 20–23 August 2025
  • Attendees: ~100 participants (visiting organising group + local participants)
  • Main activities: keynote lectures, student lightning talks, poster sessions, industry talks, and social & cultural programme

Keynote speakers

  • Mehran Ahmadlou — A subcortical switchboard for perseverative, exploratory and disengaged states
  • Bart de Strooper — Identifying the Five Key Transitions in Alzheimer’s Disease Progression
  • Colinda Scheele — Tissue resistance and oncogenic clones
  • Emre Yaksi — Searching for evolutionary traces of vertebrate thalamo-cortical systems
