August 2026 • Kavli Institute, NTNU • Trondheim, Norway

The Optical Biology Conference (OBC) is an annual, student-run meeting rotating between European host institutes. Over 3–4 days we gather PhD students, postdocs, PIs and industry partners for keynotes, student talks, poster sessions, and an industry day.

Who attends?

~60–100 participants: PhD students, postdocs, and PIs across optical microscopy, computational imaging and biology. The retreat format encourages close interactions and collaborations.

Why sponsor?

Small, highly-targeted audience + retreat format = meaningful conversations (poster hall, coffee breaks, demos). Sponsorship funds travel, accommodation, and student training.
