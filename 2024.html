Summary — Lisbon 2024

The inaugural student-organised Optical Biology Conference in 2024 brought together UCL Optical Biology students and institutional partners in Lisbon.

  • Organisers: 18 UCL students
  • Participants: ~30 attendees
  • Activities: 3 keynotes, student talks, posters, campus tours and roundtable discussions
