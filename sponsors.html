Why sponsor?

We offer sponsors direct engagement with a focused group of early-career researchers and group leaders in optical imaging, microscopy, computational imaging and biology. The retreat-style format creates high-quality networking moments.

Budget

The estimated total budget for OBC 2026 is €8,000 (upper bound €9,000). Funds cover travel & accommodation (~50%), catering (~25%), venue & AV (~15%), and social/misc (~10%).
